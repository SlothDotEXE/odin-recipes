The American Hamburger

Description

An American Classic. The hamburger is seen everywhere from sport stadiums to steakhouses. Knowing how to make one of these doesn't make you a real chef, but it definitely makes you a real American.

Ingredients

  • 1x Hamburger Bun
  • 2x Sliced Swiss Cheese
  • 1lb of Ground Beef
  • 2lb of Chopped Onion

Steps

  1. Cook the rounded meat until ready.
  2. Once the meat is prepared, place the onions onto the same pan.
  3. Whle the onions are frying, toast your buns.
  4. Once done toasting, place the onions, cheese, and beef onto the bun.
  5. Serve.
