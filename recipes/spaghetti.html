Classic Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a great, simple meal that is effective in keeping you fed.

Ingredients

  • 1x Can of Tomato Paste
  • 1x Package of Spaghetti
  • 1lb of Ground Beef
  • 1x Bottle of Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti strands until ready.
  2. Once the spaghetti is prepared, place the meatballs into the same pot.
  3. Whle the meatballs are cooking, begin placing table.
  4. Serve.
