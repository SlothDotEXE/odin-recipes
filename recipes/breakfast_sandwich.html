English Breakfast Sandwich

Description

The English Breakfast Sandwich is a classic start to a standard work day. It's delicious, cheap, and quick to make. Below we will explain the steps to make such a breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1x Rounded sausage
  • 1x Egg
  • 1x English Muffin
  • 100g of Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the sausage until ready.
  2. Once the sausage is prepared, place the egg onto the same pan.
  3. Whle the egg is frying, toast your english muffins.
  4. Once done toasting, remove muffing and place sausage and egg on it.
  5. Grate cheese to preference ontop of the sandwich.
  6. Serve.
